# Rafiki Studio — Master Pillars for the Script & Story Engine

This is the consolidated foundation map for the Rafiki Studio storytelling system.

The objective is NOT:

* screenplay formatting,
* AI script generation,
* generic writing assistance.

> A studio-grade storytelling cognition, iteration, emotional balancing, linguistic identity, and production orchestration system capable of supporting Pixar/Disney-level storytelling and beyond.
